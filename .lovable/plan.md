## How to Enter Fuel Filled Data

You're already on the right page (`/maintenance/<vehicleId>`). Here's the flow:

1. **Sidebar → Vehicle Maintenance** — opens the vehicle list.
2. **Click the vehicle** you fueled (e.g. College Bus – TN11Z4470) to open its detail page.
3. On the vehicle page, click the **"+ Add Fuel Entry"** button (top-right of the header, next to "Add Maintenance").
4. Fill the dialog:
   - **Indent Number** (required, unique) — from the Five Roads fuel station indent slip
   - **Fuel Date**
   - **Fuel Type** — Petrol / Diesel
   - **Odometer Reading** — must be higher than the previous entry for this vehicle
   - **Fuel Quantity (Litres)**
   - **Rate per Litre** — Total Amount auto-calculates
   - **Driver / Filled By**
   - **Remarks** (optional)
5. **Save** — the entry appears under the **Fuel History** tab. Mileage (km/L) is computed automatically from the previous odometer reading. Monthly and vehicle-wise totals update in the **Reports** tab.
